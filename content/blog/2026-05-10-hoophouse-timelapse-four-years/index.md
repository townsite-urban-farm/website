Since October 2022, a camera mounted inside the hoophouse has been taking a photo every two hours throughout the day.
Over four years later, that's more than 9,700 images — a record of planting, growing, and harvesting across dozens of seasons in Flagstaff's high desert climate.

We've compressed that archive into a set of stop-motion videos and a browsable photo gallery, now available on the [Timelapse](/timelapse/) page.
You can watch the full sequence or isolate a single time of day — sunrise through dusk — to see how light and seasons shape the space.

The photo gallery lets you scroll through every day at a glance, with a thumbnail for each two-hour interval.
Click any thumbnail to see a larger photo.
