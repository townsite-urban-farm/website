Rain gardens are an excellent approach for low-maintenance, passive rainwater harvesting.
Rather than collecting in a tank, which requires regular maintenance, water directed into a basin sized such that it should all seep into the ground within about 3 days.
Our rain garden is planted with native grasses and wildflowers, and as a result never needs supplemental watering since all the plants living there are adapted to our climate.
