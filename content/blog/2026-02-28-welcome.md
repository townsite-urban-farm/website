Welcome to the new Townsite Urban Farm website!
We've launched this site as a place to share updates on projects, seasonal reflections, growing guides, and anything else happening at the farm and in our community.

## What to Expect

- **Project updates** — what we're building, planting, and experimenting with
- **How-to guides** — practical knowledge from our experience with permaculture and cooperative growing
- **Community news** — events, workshops, and collaborations with partner organizations
- **Reading and resources** — books, articles, and ideas that inspire us

## Get Involved

The best way to stay connected is to [join our Slack](https://join.slack.com/t/townsiteurbanfarm/shared_invite/zt-3rhfrk4f5-C6yC6BsGHwTivDhq6uN7Ag) — that's where we coordinate, share ideas, and plan workdays.

You can also subscribe to this site to see new posts via [RSS feed](/blog/index.xml).
